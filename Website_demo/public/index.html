Vietnamese famous food

As a proud Vietnamese, I take every chance I get to introduce people to the amazing and diverse cuisine of my country. There are so many to explore, but I will introduce 3 of my favourite dishes that I think represent the cuisine of North Vietnam - where I am from

  • Bun Rieu - Vietnamese rice vermicelli with crab
  • Pho
  • Bun cha - Vietnamese vermicelli with grilled pork
